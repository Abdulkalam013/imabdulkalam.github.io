About Me

Creative Web Developer

Web development is the process of building, creating, and maintaining websites. It involves using programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design, develop, and deploy websites. Web developers are responsible for creating the visual and technical aspects of a website, ensuring that it is easily navigable and user-friendly. They work closely with web designers and other stakeholders to ensure that the website meets the needs of its users. Web developers can work in a variety of industries, including e-commerce, finance, and healthcare, and can have a range of responsibilities depending on the company or organization they work for.
